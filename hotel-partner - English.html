About Asiatravel.com Holdings Ltd

It is Asia’s leading online travel company that offers a global inventory of over 8m travel products worldwide. Its online booking feature for all-inclusive packages strongly appeals to travellers seeking convenience, instant confirmation and extra savings. Its ability to provide full travel services at packaged prices establishes its distinctive position in the online travel market.

Serving customers worldwide through 13 major language sites, its 19 offices in Asia, Middle East and Europe operate and provide 24 X 7 customer services. With comprehensive ground experience, supplier relationships, proprietary systems and operational knowledge, Asiatravel.com maintains a strong travel brand recognised for its reliability, sincerity and integrity for consumers and partners alike. Its recent award includes the Best Online Travel Agency 2015, for the 3rd consecutive year by TTG Travel Awards Asia Pacific.

Established in 1995, it is listed on the Singapore Stock Exchange since 2001. Its recently established B2B division consists of TAcentre.com and Savio-Staff-Travel, serves the travel industry and corporate sectors respectively. For more information please visit www.asiatravel.com.

Why Partner with Asiatravel.com?

I. Flexible Guest Payment Options-

Our website offers 2 payment modes for Hotel Reservations-

  1. Pay at Hotel - Hotel partner issues Selling Rates and guest Pay at Hotel directly upon check-in.
  2. Prepay - Hotel partner issues Selling Rates and guest Prepay Asiatravel.com in full prior to check-in.

II. Multi-distribution channels-

Your hotel will be included in our various distribution channels:

  1. B2C Website
  2. B2B Booking Engine
  3. Affiliates/Co-Brand websites
  4. Localised Marketing Activities- Above the line marketing such as credit card tie-ups, TV commercials on cable channels or printed media, local advertisements, retail outlets and consumer fairs.

III. Localised Customer Service and Sales Support-

Because of our network of offices across 19 offices throughout Asia, Middle East and Europe, our localized sales support team will assure your partnership with us will be maximized with diversified business opportunities and bottom line result.

IV. Trusted Travel and Hotel accommodation service provider-

We are over 20 years in this business and have been a trusted partner of many hotels, tour operators and airlines. A good majority of our clients are repeat customers who have used Asiatravel.com for all their travel needs for many years now. Thereby you can be assured of our commitment to give you the best exposure to our vast portfolio of customer-base.

Asiatravel.com is a recognised travel brand for reliability, sincerity and integrity to consumers and partners alike and earning the TTG’s Asia Pacific coveted award of Best Online Travel Agency in 2013, 2014 and 2015.
