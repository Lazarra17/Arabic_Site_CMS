About Asiatravel.com Holdings Ltd

It is Asia’s leading online travel company that offers a global inventory of over 8 million travel products worldwide. Its online booking feature for all-inclusive packages strongly appeals to travellers seeking convenience, instant confirmation and extra savings. Its ability to provide full travel services at packaged prices establishes its distinctive position in the online travel market.

Serving customers worldwide through 13 major language sites, its 17 offices in Asia, Middle East and Europe operate and provide 24 X 7 customer services. With comprehensive ground experience, supplier relationships, proprietary systems and operational knowledge, Asiatravel.com maintains a strong travel brand recognised for its reliability, sincerity and integrity for consumers and partners alike. Its recent award includes the Best Online Travel Agency 2015, for the 3rd consecutive year by TTG Travel Awards Asia Pacific.

Established in 1995, it is listed on the Singapore Stock Exchange since 2001. Its recently established B2B division consists of TAcentre.com and Savio-Staff-Travel, serves the travel industry and corporate sectors respectively. For more information please visit www.asiatravel.com 

Our Products

All-Inclusive Travel Packages

A leading online travel product is our all-inclusive package booking feature that provides travellers, even on a last minute, the ability to book all the necessary travel components in a hassle free booking experience for that perfect holiday trip.

Once the destination and preferred dates of travel are selected, the system will present all the packages available in different program combinations of tours, visit to theme parks or attractions, transfer options and other activities available for that destination. For each package, the system will recommend the lowest flight fare available but the user can still select any preferred airline and flight schedule available, a hotel of the category that suits personal preference or budget and finally the option to decide the order of dates for the tours or activities. It is a ready-made travel package that yet caters to every individual preference and budget. Each time you search, the dynamic engine behind this packaging system connects all our suppliers for the most current pricing and product availability.

Our Accommodation Portfolio

A selection of over 200,000 hotels, resorts, hostels, lodges and inns at over 4,800 cities in over 180 countries worldwide ensures you always have the choice to choose the right accommodation for your trip. Relevant but important information of each property are displayed so that you get the necessary product information. We ensure all our accommodation operators provide their full range of room types, rate plans, promotions and offers in our system so that you are always receiving the best deals.

Our Transportation Portfolio

Our flight engine searches real time flight schedules and fares of over 500 leading airlines of each flight sector available so that you are always assured of getting to your desired destination. Multiple destinations can also be booked within one transaction as the system searches for the best connections and combined fares even from different airlines. You will find the most user friendly scroll fare table for a quick overview of available airlines and current fares before you decide on the schedules. This unique display feature is also extended into the packages section where you can quickly combine a flight with other components like hotels and destination activities into a singular transaction.

Other transportation services available include self-drive car rental, ferry tickets, coach rental and airport shuttles.

Our Activities Portfolio

Booking sightseeing and tour activities together flights and accommodation as a package is highly recommended as there are additional savings. But sometimes it is difficult to decide in advance, so we have specially created this option for you to book separately from an extensive range of tours, tickets into theme parks and attractions, private or shared transportation, and a whole range of other touristic activities in over 50 countries worldwide. Many of these activities can be booked just a day in advance and some even for the same day.

Our Service Guarantee & Customer Care

We provide 24 X 7 customer care with local number calls from over 30 countries worldwide. Customer care information is provided on every travel voucher. Our Best Price Guarantee assures you get the best value while our Cash Rewards recognises your return patronage. Further information are available on this site in each respective page.
